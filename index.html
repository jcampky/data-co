Instructions:

please note: this generator only works with public works, not locked fics. to input the data of a locked fic manually, please go here.

  1. go to your ao3 history and copy the link of the fic you want to record as read
  2. paste the link to the fic in the field below
  3. click generate
  4. continue adding fics until you've put all of them that you've read
  5. click finish and save your data
